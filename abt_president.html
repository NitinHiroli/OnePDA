About President

Dr. Bhimashankar C. Bilgundi

President, HKE Society

Dr. Bhimashankar Bilgundi comes from a highly respected family with concern for the betterment of the society. The quintessence of his personality represents the vision and mission of his Father Late Sri Chandrashekhar Bilgundi and Mother Late Smt. Kasturbai. The Social activism and proactive approach to civic problems can be traced to dynamism of his father Sri Chandrashekar Bilgundi who was the President of Town Municipality, patronized, Cooperative movement and was Governing Council Member of HKE Society.

Dr. Bhimashankar Bilgundi is an alumni of M.R. Medical College, Kalaburagi. After his graduation in Medicine, he kept himself active in Medical Education and later on in social activism and business. The vision to build a new healthy society through Political participation is witnessed through - Treasurer District Congress Committee, Karnataka Pradesh Congress Committee Member, Member Divisional Railway Users Committee Solapur and Member Zonal Railway Users Committee, Mumbai.

The quest to become a 'Face in the Crowd', he used his exemplary strength in organizing human resource and bringing people together with a dream to develop Hyderabad Karnataka through Education.
